Mobile Phone Market is at its peak, and there are many phones that you can buy for under 15000 INR. This article will help you identify the best phones under 15000 INR. We start with the Value For Money phone, the best value for money phones under 15000.

Poco M2 PRO ( Value For Money )
Price – 12999/- INR

This mobile Smartphone is an intelligent Pack Value device that provides a lot of value to its customers.

Poco M2 Pro is an All-Rounder device known for its good features and camera.

It has a Quad camera setup with a 48-megapixel + 8-megapixel ultrawide + 5 megapixel Mac + 2-megapixel depth camera.

Poco M2 Pro has Qualcomm Snapdragon 720G, usually found in high-rang mobile devices that cost around 20 to 25 thousand rupees.

This Phone comes in three intelligent variants :

  • 4GB Ram 64GB ROM
  • 6GB ram 64GB ROM
  • 6GB Ram 128 GB ROM

This Phone has a big 6.67-inch FHD+ display with Triple Corning Gorilla Glass 5. A Glass back and a slim bezel can provide you a superb feel while using the device.

A massive 5000mah battery with 33 W fast charging and a side-mounted fingerprint is a one-up to the device.

If you’re planning to buy this device, make sure you have checked the remaining ones before going forward.

Next on the list is a Realme smartphone.

Realme Narzo 20 Pro ( Best Charging Beast )
Price – 13999/- INR

This Phone has a unique specialty. It has a special 65W fast charging which is not usually found on any mobile phone, and a battery of 4500 mah. Realme has claimed that it charges 0% to 100% in just 38 mins (that’s super fast ) and can stay on up to 28 days on standby.

A 3 min charge can give you enough power to play 10 hours of music.

Suppose you’re buying a phone because you need a good battery. Narzo 20 comes with superfast charging and a good battery hold capacity.

But this Phone is more than a battery; it has a 6.5″ 90Hz smooth FHD+ display with a 90% screen to body ratio Cornering Gorilla Glass.

Realme Narzo 20 Pro has a MediaTek Helio g95 processor with 6GB Ram and 64GB ROM, making it viable for gaming and providing a superfast smooth experience.

Realme Narzo 20 Pro comes with a Quad camera setup that is 48MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP

This Phone is usually known for its fast charging feature, so if you’re looking for a smartphone with a good battery and can charge itself fast, it’s the Narzo 20 Pro.

The next Phone on the list is

Redmi Note 10 ( Best Mid-Range Phone )
Price 12999/- INR

REDMI PHONES ARE ALWAYS KNOWN FOR THEIR OFFERINGS.

You can always expect more while buying a Redmi Phone, and Redmi phones have proved it every time.

Redmi Note 10 is a mid-range beast that will offer a wide range of features.

Redmi Note 10 has a Snapdragon 678 processor, a smooth GPU for gaming, and a 6.43″ Super AMOLED punch-hole display at the top center.

Redmi Note 10 also has a 48-megapixel Quad camera set up ( 48Mp + 8Mp + 2Mp + 2Mp ) and a 5000 mah battery with 33-watt of fast charging and Stereo speakers.

It comes in 4GB 64GB and 6GB 128GB variants, a very well-designed phone that gives a premium feel. In Short, another value for money device.

Next on the list is another well-known device.

Poco X3 ( Best Performance )
Price 14500/- INR

Poco X3 comes with the best mid-range processor, Snapdragon 732G with 6GB Ram and 128 GB storage, making it a powerhouse at that price range.

Not only that, it has a 6.67″ FHD+ display with a 120HZ refresh rate which is not easily found on a mid-range device. A 91.25% screen-to-body ratio gives a bezel-less feel to the Phone and 6000mah battery and 33W Quick charging, usually found in most devices these days.

Talking about performance, Poco X3 is a mid-range beast. So you’re getting an excellent performing device with a combination of 120Hz display, Snapdragon 726G processor, and dual stereo speaker at the most negligible price value.

This is a real good option if you’re looking for phones under 15000/- INR

Last on our list is……

Realme 8 ( Killer Looks Big Battery )
Price – 14,999

Realme 8 is a lightweight super-slim device with a 6.4 FHD+ AMOLED display with a 90% screen-to-body ratio.

A 64-megapixel AI quad-camera setup ( 64MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP )

Realme 8 comes with an Helio G95 gaming processor for a smooth and fast experience.

This Phone is an excellent package with an in-display fingerprint for 14999/-
