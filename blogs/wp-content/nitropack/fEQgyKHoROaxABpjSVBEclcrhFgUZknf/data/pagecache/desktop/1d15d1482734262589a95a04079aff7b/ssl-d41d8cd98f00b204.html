ONEPLUS NORD CE 5G

The first one we have here is ONEPLUS NORD CE 5G. It gives us an option to choose from three eye-catching colors: blue void, charcoal ink, and silver ray. This sleek design phone has a 6.43 inches FLUID AMOLED display with a 1080 x 2400-pixel resolution.

It comes with an excellent 6 GB Ram and a Qualcomm Snapdragon 750G processor for smooth running. This fantastic phone has a multi-touch touch screen. It has a non-removable 4500 mAh battery that can last all day long.


It provides us with massive internal storage of 128 GB. Moreover, it has an optical fingerprint sensor. Talking of the camera, it provides us with three rear cameras: the primary camera having a resolution of 64 MP and the supporting cameras having resolutions of 8 MP and 2 MP. The front camera has a resolution of 16 MP.
Such a spectacular phone, and you might think of it as an expensive one? Well, happy to disappoint you. This phone is only priced at Rs. 22,999. Surprising, isn’t it? this is the first phone on 10 Best Phones In August 2021

SAMSUNG GALAXY F62

The next one we have here is one of our personal favorites, the Samsung Galaxy F62. The price ranges from Rs.17,149 to Rs.23,999, depending on where you buy it from. Affordable, right? Furthermore, it comes in 3 stunning colors: Laser green, Laser Blue, and Laser grey.


This phone has a massive 6.7 inches, 393 PPI super AMOLED Plus display with 1080 x 2400 pixels screen resolution. The screen is multi-touch with a fingerprint sensor and is protected by Corning Gorilla glass v3 to prevent it from breaking even after falls and tired of your phone running out of charging every hour? This model comes with a gigantic battery of 7000 mAh and provides you with a talk time of up to 50 hours.

No need to carry your charger everywhere you go. It has the Samsung Exynos 9 Octa 9825 processor exclusively made for gaming. It has a bright display. It has a 6 GB Ram.


It has four rear cameras and one front camera. The rear cameras have different resolutions: the primary wide-angle camera with a resolution of 64 MP, a 12 MP ultra-wide-angle supporting camera, and two 5 MP supporting cameras, one being the depth and one being the macro camera. It is a dual sim phone.

REALME 8 PRO

Another one on our list is Realme 8 Pro. This incredible piece has a 6.4 inches screen with a high resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels. It also comes with a fingerprint sensor positioned on the front screen. The RAM is 6 GB, and the internal storage is 128 GB.

It has four rear cameras and one front camera. The primary camera has one of the highest resolutions that a mobile phone can offer, I.e., 108 MP, to record your sacred memories. The other rare cameras have resolutions of 8 MP, 2 MP, and 2 MP. The front camera gives you a spectacular 16 MP resolution.


This one houses a massive capacity battery of 4500 mAh. We get to choose from 3 beautiful colors: infinite blue, infinite black, and illuminating yellow. It retails between Rs. 17,999 to Rs. 21,800 (price depends upon the site you buy from).

VIVO IQOO Z3

Another one we have for you is VIVO IQoo Z3, launched in June 2021. Its price is 19,900, and you would be surprised to see its features at this low cost.

It is a triple camera phone, the resolution of the main camera being 64 MP. The other two primary cameras have resolutions of 8 MP and 2 MP. Another exciting feature of this phone is its 10 x Digital zoom, auto flash, and face detection. The front camera has an excellent resolution of 16 MP.


It has a 6.58 inches display with a resolution of 1080 x 2408 pixels. It comes with a built-in flash LED and a fingerprint sensor. This phone offers a 4400 mAh battery that can last up to 18 hours. It is a dual sim phone supporting 2G, 3G, 4G, and the latest 5G. It offers you two stunning colors, ace black and cyber blue.

XIAOMI POCO M3

For all those looking for a pocket-friendly option, here we have for you is XIAOMI POCO M3. It retails at Rs.12,990, and the features it is offering are spectacular. It has plenty of internal storage of 64 GB. A 6000 mAh battery to last all day and a 6.53 inches screen with a very high resolution.


It also offers three excellent cameras, with the highest resolution being 48 MP and the other two supporting cameras having a resolution of 2 MP. The front camera has a resolution of 8 MP. It comes in 3 eye-catching colors power black, coal blue, and poco yellow. It provides you dual sim facility with a network it supports 2G, 3G, and 4G.

SAMSUNG GALAXY M32

Another one of our favorites is the SAMSUNG GALAXY M32. It is an incredible model at a very affordable price that is just Rs.14,999. It comes with four rare cameras.

The primary camera has the highest resolution of 64 MP, and the other supporting cameras have a lesser resolution of one is 8 MP and the other 2 being 2 MP. The front camera comes with a resolution of 20 MP.


Here we have a display of 6.4 inches and a long-lasting battery with 6000 mAh. It also has an LED flash and autofocus. And it also comes in unique colors, black and light blue.

XIAOMI MI 11 LITE

The XIAOMI MI 11 LITE that retails for Rs.22,950 is also an excellent option for you to consider. We can select from three colors: Tuscany coral, jazz blue, and vinyl black. It has a RAM of 6 GB and ROM of 128 GB. It has a reliable battery power of 4250 mAh. It has a large display of 6.55 inches. The resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels. Another one of its great features is it is very lightweight.


Good news for all the clumsy people reading this, this device comes with a waterproof feature. The fingerprint sensor is present on the screen for easy access. The number of rare cameras is three, one of them being 64 MP and the supportive ones being 8 MP and 5 MP. The first camera has a resolution of 16 MP and also has face detection.

Realme 8 5G

The Realme 8 5G offers us a 4 GB ram and a 128 GB ROM. It has a lasting battery of 5000 mAh. It has a 6.5 inches high-resolution screen. Moreover, it has an


ISOCELL Plus sensor as well as a side-positioned fingerprint sensor. Like most of the options discussed before, it also has three rare cameras and one front camera. The 48 MP primary camera allows face detection and “touch to focus.” The two 2 MP accessory cameras allow a 10-x digital zoom and an auto flash.


This Realme model is a dual sim device supporting 2G, 3G, and 4G networks. We can choose from 2 elegant colors a supersonic blue and supersonic black. It is a budget-friendly phone which costs only Rs.15,950.

XIAOMI REDMI 9 POWER

The cheapest phone on our list today is XIAOMI REDMI 9 POWER retailing at Rs.10,999. It has a CPU octa-core processor that is ideal for gaming. It comes with a 6.53 inches screen display with 1080 x 2340 pixels.

The RAM is 4 GB, and the internal storage is 128 GB. It has dual sim slots supporting 2G, 3G, and 4G networks and a fingerprint sensor on the side. It has four back cameras. The main one being 48 MP and the supporting being 8 MP 2 MP and 2 MP.


The battery is 6000 mAh that can last all day long. It comes in 4 unique colors: mighty black, fiery red, electric green, and blazing blue.

XIAOMI REDMI NOTE 10S

Last but not least, we have the XIAOMI REDMI NOTE 10S, which costs Rs.14,999. It has 6 GB RAM and a 64 GB ROM. It has four rare cameras. The highest being 64 MP and the others being 8 MP, 2 MP, and 2 MP. It has a 13 MP front camera.

It has a 6.43 inches large display with a resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels. It comes in 3 eye-catching colors: shadow black, frost white, and deep-sea blue. It has a long-lasting 5000 mAh battery.


Moreover, it has a LED flash, a digital zoom, face detection, and touch to focus. It has dual sim slots, and a side positioned fingerprint sensor. the last phone 10 Best Phones In August 2021
